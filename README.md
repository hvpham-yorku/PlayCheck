# PlayCheck – Vision Statement

# Summary
PlayCheck is a low-cost, mobile-based sports support system for referees, players, and organizers in university intramural and amateur sports. It improves fairness, coordination, and transparency by integrating tools for video review, scheduling, communication, and league data access into one platform.

# Project Description
PlayCheck supports the full lifecycle of amateur sporting events—from scheduling to officiating and historical analysis. Referees can review live or recorded footage to assist in making accurate calls. Players can track participation and view statistics. Organizers can coordinate assignments and manage matches. League data such as standings, results, and team performance are also accessible. By combining officiating tools with communication and league management features, PlayCheck helps reduce disputes, improve organization, and enhance the experience for all users.

# Target Users
- University intramural sports programs
- Amateur and recreational referees
- Players in community leagues
- Tournament organizers and event coordinators

# Project Value
PlayCheck delivers value through four core capabilities:
1. Officiating Support: Referees can review clips or live feeds to verify calls, improving confidence without replacing their authority.
2. Player Visibility: Players can access game history and statistics to track performance and participation.
3. Scheduling & Coordination: Users can view match calendars; organizers can invite participants and assign referees.
4. League Transparency: Team standings, statistics, and rankings are visible, boosting engagement and competitiveness.

By focusing on mobile devices and simple workflows, the app can be used directly on the field with minimal disruption. PlayCheck also supports multiple sports, making it flexible for leagues that host a variety of activities.

# Success Criteria
The system will be considered successful if:
1. Referees can upload and review video footage linked to games.
2. Players can view past matches and personal participation history.
3. Users can access a calendar of upcoming games.
4. Organizers can communicate with referees and manage assignments.
5. League standings and statistics are clearly visible in the app.
6. Events and tournaments can be organized entirely within the system.

Overall, PlayCheck aims to improve fairness, consistency, and enjoyment in amateur sports by making video review, league management, and event coordination simple, affordable, and accessible.
